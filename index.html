Reviews & Revisions

v2.0 → v2.1

ISSUE — The constant residual (α_R) was not observable: a universal shift identical across all clocks is indistinguishable from a redefinition of units.

RESOLUTION — v2.1 introduces the differential drift (ε_R): differences between clock types according to their coherence structure — a signature measurable in principle, with bound |Δε_R| ≲ 10⁻⁸.

About

I am Mateo Moreira, an independent researcher from Uruguay. T≡M did not begin as a paper: it began as a childhood memory — hopping across the same-colored tiles of a sidewalk — and became words for the first time in an email written in 2018.

Everything that followed — the theory, its revisions, the Companion Paper, Coherent Data — is published with a DOI, including the corrections. This site gathers all of it in one place.
